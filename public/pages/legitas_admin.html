Legislative Information Tracking and Administration System (LegITAS) is a custom-built data and content management system designed to streamline the handling of legislative documents such as ordinances and resolutions. The application offers a centralized platform for the creation, updating, scheduling, and viewing of both draft and approved legislative materials. LegITAS aims to modernize and digitize the legislative workflow, replacing cumbersome manual processes with a structured and efficient system tailored to the needs of government offices and legislative bodies.

The core functionality of LegITAS revolves around the comprehensive management of ordinances and resolutions. Users can effortlessly create draft versions, edit and update them as needed, and track each document through its legislative journey—from initial drafting to first, second, and third readings. The system clearly shows the current status of each item, indicating whether it has been approved, deferred, or is still under review. This real-time visibility enhances transparency and ensures that staff can make informed decisions and follow up appropriately.

Beyond tracking, LegITAS simplifies the administrative tasks that often accompany legislative work. Users can easily generate print-ready versions of ordinances, resolutions, endorsements, and other related documents. The platform also supports scheduling features to help users stay on top of sessions and readings. With its user-friendly interface and organized structure, LegITAS enables both technical and non-technical users to improve efficiency, reduce errors, and maintain better coordination throughout the legislative process.
